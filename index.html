About





Claim the Kingdom is a VR based survival game which is powered by Unity Game Engine and HTC Vive. The game starts with a player trapped in a room. The motive of the player is to survive in that room by taking the specific color magic spells and at the same time avoiding other colors spells. The player has to survive in the room for three minutes and he would be given three lives. So if a player gets hit by the spell of other colors, he would loose a life and if that happens three times, then the player will die. As the game progresses, the speed at which the spells are coming will increase.
