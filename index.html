Creating a Dark Room and Moving Spells inside the room

Progress

The game requires a dark room with firewalls illuminating the parts of room as they travel towards the player. We have picked assets for room and fireballs, integrated with vive and further looked into the movement of the fireball towards and away from player. Right now, we have camera as the player and a single fireball moving in one direction towards player. We have tested rotation, transformation and translation effects on the fireballs to create an immersive environment.

Next Steps

To add rigid bodies and create an effect of collision when the fireball hits the player. To add multiple fireballs from various directions in the scene. To further work on the aesthetics and game logic.

Challenges

  • To take the position of the player dynamically and change the fireball directions towards the new position of player.
  • Deciding on audio effects.

About





Claim the Kingdom is a VR based survival game which is powered by Unity Game Engine and HTC Vive. The game starts with a player trapped in a room. The motive of the player is to survive in that room by taking the specific color magic spells and at the same time avoiding other colors spells. The player has to survive in the room for three minutes and he would be given three lives. So if a player gets hit by the spell of other colors, he would loose a life and if that happens three times, then the player will die. As the game progresses, the speed at which the spells are coming will increase.
